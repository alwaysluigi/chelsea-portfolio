About

Chelsea Friel is a graphic designer currently living in Austin, Texas. She specializes in branding, web design, and animation. When she’s not obsessing over type and character walk cycles, she’s seeking out the next horror movie to give her nightmares.

She lives with her nine foot tall partner Matthew, three ungrateful cats, and a ghoulish dog who is trying her hardest to put her into an early grave. If she were to choose anyone to portray her in a movie, it would be Brendan Gleeson, without a doubt. They have the same ears, and the likeness cannot be denied.
